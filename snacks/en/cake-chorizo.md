# Chorizo loaf cake

## Ingredients

  - 3 eggs
  - 230g flour
  - 1 sachet of baking powder
  - 15 cl milk
  - 100g gruyère (or mozzarella)
  - 200g chorizo

## Steps

 1. Preheat the oven to 180°C
 2. In a bowl, pour in all the ingredients
 3. Stir everything together
 4. Pour into a buttered loaf tin
 5. Bake for 30 to 45 min

 ## Tips
 - Add a tablespoon of olive oil
