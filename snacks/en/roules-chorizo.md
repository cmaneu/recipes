# Chorizo pinwheels

## Ingredients

- One puff pastry
- 1/3 of a mild chorizo
- 35g grated gruyère
- 1/3 of a small jar of tomato sauce (Barilla)
- 1 egg

## Preparation

1. Preheat the oven to 180°
2. Unroll the pastry
3. Blend the chorizo, the gruyère, the egg and the tomato sauce
4. Spread the mixture over the pastry
5. Roll everything into a log, then put it in the freezer for 5 min
6. Cut the pastry into 1cm slices and place them on a baking sheet
7. Bake for 10 min
