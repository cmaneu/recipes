# Pizza dough

## Ingredients

- 250g of flour
- 150ml of lukewarm water
- 15g of fresh yeast (or 7-8g of baker's yeast),
- 1 tsp of salt
- 0.5 tsp of sugar
- 1 tbsp of olive oil

## Steps

- In a bowl, mix the yeast, 60mL of lukewarm water, the sugar and 3 tablespoons of flour.
- Let it rest for 30 minutes.
- In another bowl, mix the rest of the flour with the salt and the rest of the lukewarm water and add the previous mixture.
- Knead for 10-15 minutes, then let it rest in a warm place for 2 hours.
- When you roll out the dough, you can let it rest again for 15 minutes once rolled out.
- When you make your pizza, allow 12 to 15 minutes of baking at 180°C
