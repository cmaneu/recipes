# Fresh pasta

## Dough (for 1 person)

### Ingredients

80g of wheat flour
20g of fine durum wheat semolina (De Cecco?)
1 egg

### Preparation
- Mix the flour and the semolina
- Place it on a board, make a well and put the egg in the centre
- Beat the egg with a fork (and a tablespoon in the other hand, in case the egg escapes)
- Gradually incorporate the flour into the mixture
- Form the dough into a ball and knead it for a few minutes
- Wrap it in cling film and let it rest for 15 min

- Divide the dough into several parts (2 parts per 100g) and run it through the machine.

# Example of filling

# Butter and basil sauce
- Melt the butter in a pan

# Porcini sauce
- Melt the butter in a pan
- Add the garlic
- Add the porcini mushrooms
- After 10 min of cooking, add liquid crème fraîche
- Add the drained pasta, a little cooking water and some parsley
