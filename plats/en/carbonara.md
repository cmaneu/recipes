# Pasta alla Carbonara

## Ingredients

- 125g of long pasta per person (Voirello, De Cecco, or Barilla) - Linguine, Mafaldine, Lasagnette, Tagliatelle
- Parmesan (whole, to grate fresh, Parmigiano Reggiano)
- 1 egg per person + 2 eggs for the dish
- Guanciale, or Pancetta (enough :)
- Pepper, Salt, Olive oil

## Steps

- In a pot, put a large amount of water (NO SALT!), and bring to a boil
- In another container, put the egg yolks, a little salt, plenty of pepper and grated parmesan
- Beat everything with a mixer (a light foam appears)
- In a frying pan, fry the roughly sliced meat. It should brown well. Remove it from the heat and set it aside.
- Once the water is boiling, add a handful of coarse salt, then immediately the pasta
- Add 1 to 2 tablespoons of the cooking water to the egg yolks, and beat again
- Once the pasta is cooked and drained, pour everything into the container and mix
- Add the fried meat on top of the plate
