# Sweet crêpes

## Ingredients

For 15ish portions:

- 50g of butter
- 4 eggs
- 2 spoon serving of sugar
- 1 pinch of salt
- 250g of flour
- 1/2 liter of milk
- 4 cc of "grand marnier" (a french orange-flavoured cognac liquor, you can do it with an old rum too)

## Preparation

- Sift the flour
- Add the sugar and the salt
- Add the eggs one by one
- Add the milk little by little
- Let the dough rest for a few hours (2-4h)

Then cook the crêpes over low heat

# Nico's sweet crêpes

## Ingredients
- 1kg of flour
- 12 eggs
- 1 litre of milk
- 1 beer
- Lemon / orange zest

## Preparation
- Sift the flour
- Add the eggs before the flour
- Top up with the milk
- Add the beer
- Let it rest
- Add water if needed so the batter is liquid
